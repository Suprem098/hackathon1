ABOUT BCA

This website is designed to provide resources and information for BCA students. The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a 4-year (8-semester) undergraduate program offered by several universities in Nepal, including Tribhuvan University (TU), Purbanchal University, Pokhara University, Mid-Western University, and others. This program is designed to equip students with a strong foundation in computer science, application development, and information technology. The BCA program focuses on both theoretical and practical aspects of computing. It prepares students for careers in software development, web and mobile application development, database administration, networking, cybersecurity, and IT support services. The course structure also includes soft skills, communication, and project work to ensure holistic development.
